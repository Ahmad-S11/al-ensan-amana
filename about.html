About Al Ensan Amana

Al Ensan Amana is a humanitarian association working across Lebanon to support vulnerable communities through education, healthcare, and social protection.

Our Mission

To empower individuals, protect children, support women, promote equality, and respond to humanitarian crises with dignity and professionalism.

Our Vision

A just and inclusive society where everyone can live with dignity, security, and access to essential services regardless of background.
